Who am i?

My name is Robert, but my artist name is EmRo. I'm 31 and grew up in a small village in Moldova. I moved to Switzerland, Frauenfeld a year ago.


Drawing was my favorite hobby from childhood on. At the age of 17 I’ve build my first tattoo machine and the same day, I tested it on myself for the very first time.
It changed my whole life!


In 2013 I started to work in a tattoo studio and gained a lot of experience since then.
My favorite tattoo style is realism. I love tattooing portraits, skulls, movie scenes and superheroes. I always ask my clients to give me the freedom of creativity and a piece of their skin, and in return I will be willing to give them a piece of my soul.
